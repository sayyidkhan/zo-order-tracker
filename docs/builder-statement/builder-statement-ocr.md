## zo-build-challenge-how-it-works.png

HOW IT WORKS

One month. One theme. One build.

DAY 1

Theme drops

A fresh theme lands on the 1st of every month. One word, one vibe, one month to build.

DAYS 1-25

Build season

Vibe-code with Zo. Build something that relates to the theme of the month - a site, a tool, a song, a thing only you would make.

DAY 25

Submissions due

Drop a 60-second video walkthrough + a link to the build. Submitted through the dashboard, Contra-style.

END OF MONTH

Winners announced

The Zo team reviews every submission and announces 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place at the end of the month.
